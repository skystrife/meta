Detailed Description

Performs information gain feature selection: \( IG(t, c_i) = \sum_{c\in\{c_i,\overline{c_i}\}}\sum_{t'\in\{t,t'\}}P(t',c)\log\frac{P(t',c)}{P(t')P(c)} \).
